How to Play

Guess the correct word of the day in 6 attempts, get some pie!

Use the keyboard arrow buttons to navigate through the individual letters of current turn

    Green: Correct letter, correct position
    Orange: Correct letter, incorrect position
    Grey: Letter is not in word
